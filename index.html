QRHistory

Privacy Policy

QRHistory is designed to work locally on your device and keep your scan history private.

Data Handling

  • QRHistory stores scan history only on your device.
  • QRHistory does not require an account.
  • QRHistory does not upload scan history to a backend server.
  • QRHistory does not provide cloud sync.
  • QRHistory does not include analytics in the current release build.

Permissions

  • Camera: used to scan QR codes.
  • Vibration: used to give short feedback after a successful scan.

Data Sharing

  • QRHistory does not sell or share personal data.
  • When you tap actions like open in browser, email, SMS, maps, share, or dial, Android hands the content to the app you selected.
